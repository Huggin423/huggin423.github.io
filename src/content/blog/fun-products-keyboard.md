# First Impressions

The Keychron Q1 Pro is a beast. The aluminum chassis feels incredibly premium and heavy. It stays planted on my desk no matter how aggressively I type.

## The Typing Experience

I opted for the **Banana switches**, which provide a nice tactile bump without being too loud. The gasket mount design gives the board a soft, bouncy feel that makes typing a joy.

### Pros

*   ✅ Solid aluminum build
*   ✅ Wireless connectivity works flawlessly
*   ✅ VIA support for easy remapping
*   ✅ Great stock stabilizers

### Cons

*   ❌ Stock keycaps are just okay
*   ❌ Battery life could be better with RGB on

## Customization

I swapped the keycaps for a set of GMK clones with a Japanese sublegend to match my anime aesthetic. It looks stunning!

![Keyboard Setup](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1587829741301-dc798b91dd55?q=80&w=1000&auto=format&fit=crop)

## Verdict

For the price, it's hard to beat the Q1 Pro. It's a great entry point into the world of custom mechanical keyboards.
