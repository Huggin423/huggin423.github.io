# Arriving in Kyoto

Kyoto has always been on my bucket list. The blend of ancient tradition and modern life is mesmerizing.

## Fushimi Inari Taisha

We started our day early at Fushimi Inari to avoid the crowds. The thousands of vermilion torii gates are a sight to behold.

> "Photography is the story I fail to put into words." — Destin Sparks

## Gear Used

*   **Camera**: Sony A7IV
*   **Lens**: 24-70mm f/2.8 GM II

## The Food

You can't visit Kyoto without trying the Matcha. We stopped at a small tea house in Gion and had the best Matcha parfait of my life.

I took over 500 photos on this trip, but here are a few of my favorites. The lighting during the golden hour was absolutely perfect for capturing the textures of the old wooden temples.
